Search Tips

General

  • Searching for multiple words at the same time will look for matches with that exact phrase - e.g. marine mammal will only return matches mentioning marine mammal - marine and mammal will return results that mention both of the independent words marine and mammal.
  • Search filters are looking for matches in the science metadata for each data set. Any text or metadata contained in the data files (e.g. .csv table, .zip folder) will not be searched.

Year

Use the slider or type in a year number and limit your search to data that was collected and/or published between that year range. You can filter results by year in two ways:

  • Year(s) of data coverage
    : Year that data was collected
  • Year of publication
    : Year that data was published. If the data creator did not provide a publish date, then the year the data was uploaded is used instead.
